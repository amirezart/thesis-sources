## **DIGITAL CODE AS AN OBJECT GRAPHIC DESIGN FOR DISSEMINATION AND PROMOTION OF BRAND** 

**Summary.** The article reveals the features of the graphic language of coded information by identifying, systematizing, and combining QR-codes and AR-codes at the present stage of graphic design development. The analysis of coded information in different types of graphic design is presented: advertising, environmental, motion, print, packaging, set prop, web, mobile app, art, and illustration graphic design. The role of a graphic designer in the development of digital codes of various types for various fields of application is described. It is proved that the coded information is a trigger between the online and offline worlds. The article also reveals the importance of using coded information for the formation, development, and improvement of the brand. 

**Keywords:** coded information, graphic design, brand, dynamic changes, QR-code, AR-code, digital code, augmented reality. 

## **INTRODUCTION** 

Visualization technologies are rapidly improving and penetrating human life and society. During a historically short period, humanity has gone from engraving to modern digital technologies. Until recently, coded information was used only by individual brands, but the pandemic has changed the situation. And today, codes have a real purpose, because “contactlessness” has become a necessity. 

The dissemination of coded information during the COVID-19 pandemic contributes to new changes. In our times, codes are widely used for contactless registration, payments, ticket purchases, ordering, etc. They are accessible, easy to use, and safe in their properties. Also, codes do not require physical contact and are decoded by the phone camera. 

It is impossible to imagine the life of a modern person without visual data. Most companies are transforming to meet the consumers’ needs and preserve their identity, considering that the rapid technological changes in society have made information content a primary resource for brand formation. 

In this case, graphic design is the driving force, because the code contains visual elements that form potential customers’ first impression of the 

brand and conveys its values. As a result, there is a need to create a simple intuitive design code and its information content. By using coded information, it is possible to create an interactive and intuitive brand. That is why today new trends and innovations are constantly being formed in graphic design, which determined the _relevance of the study_ . 

In her paper, Teuta Cata studies the effective use of coded information and the optimal areas for its placement[1] but does not consider the compositional properties of the code. 

Oleksandra Kolisnyk, Marina Kolosnichenko, and Kalina Pashkevych consider modern graphic design as a phenomenon of social communication.[2] Meanwhile authors Maria Vorobchuk and Natalia Skliarenko in their article study the principles of design-ing the signals of visual communication of coded information,[3] although the structural component of the code is described only in general. QR codes are more effective than information on paper: this was found in a study conducted by Jennifer Fishbein, Kimberly Lau, Stephen Barone[4] and as a result, it gave impetus to the use of encoded information in graphic design.

In recent research related to the period of the COVID-19 pandemic, the authors Joshua Baugh, Oran emphasize the facilitation of information communication through the design of QR stickers facilitating information communication through the design of QR stickers and posters based on the behavioral principles of expressiveness.[5]

While studying scientific articles, textbooks, and internet resources, an attempt has been made to get acquainted with the problems of using coded information in the design. As a result, it has been found that more research on coded information in graphic design is needed. 

_Materials._ The art of graphic design directs the transmission of a specific message through visual forms of communication. It can be seen in advertising, books, magazines, and online media. Nowadays, there are many types of graphic design that focus on creating a harmonious and effective communication environment. 

Coded information and its visual perception are increasingly being studied in graphic design. The use of codes as brand identifiers is becoming an innovative tool that provides information on products or services. 

_Methods._ Analytical and structural methods, as well as an integrated approach, are used in the study. Also, a comparative analysis is used as one of the tools of the art history method, and visual methods of cognition are used, which are extremely relevant today. 

_Scientific novelty_ **:** the article presents coded information as an innovative tool for brand transformation, the impact of which can be observed through the following types of graphic design: advertising, environmental, motion, print, packaging, set prop, mobile app, art and illustration graphic design. 

_The purpose of the study_ is to divide the coded information by main types of graphic design as tool for brand transformation. 

The goal determines the following tasks: 1) to determine the main types of graphic design, where coded information is used; 2) to investigate the use of QR-codes and AR-codes at the current stage of the development of society; 3) find out what the role of a graphic designer is in the creation of a digital code and what tools designers use to increase the recognition and uniqueness of digital codes. 

## **RESULTS OF THE STUDY AND DISCUSSION** 

A sign of modern art is its desire for innovations. For today, the creation of a work of art, which directly requires the participation of the audience to reproduce the holistic idea of the designer, is relevant. Usually, such works are an imaginary interpretation of the work of art because they provide the opportunity to change the viewer`s mind, to supplement the imagination with new images. Instead, the viewer`s influence on the art object is reduced to his or her passive assessment.[11] 

**132** 

Modern understanding of innovations involves the viewer’s full interaction with the work of art. The viewer can really influence the work of art and physically transform it, acquiring the role of co-author. The essence of using the innovative tool in the brand transformation is the transition from a passive attitude to active consumer interaction with the art object. Today it is possible to find interactive shop windows and billboards, wall-touchscreens, etc. In this wide list of innovative tools, the use of augmented reality technologies in advertising is a promising direction. 

The use of augmented reality technology (AR-code) has become possible due to its close connection with matrix codes (QR-codes), which have become widespread during the COVID-19 pandemic. QR-codes are more than a work of art, they are a powerful digital tool that allows customers to receive various information on a product simply by scanning it with a mobile phone camera. People use matrix codes in their daily life to browse food menus, pay bills, get more information, and so on. 

Nowadays coded information is a tool in which not only appearance but also the content are important. That is why brands are looking for new methods and applying augmented reality when decoding code. AR-code (augmented reality code) is a QR-code with a marker for AR.js, which redirects the user to the AR web application or mobile application. AR-codes allow adding an extra dimension to the product, creating interactive content for the real world. 

With the advent of QR-codes in augmented reality, the viewer has the opportunity to scan AR-code and interact with the content in one moment. The process is the same for both the QR-code and the AR-code. Both types of coded information are related to the specific place or environment in which they are located.

The peculiarity of developing the design of coded information in graphic design is determined by the principles of its creation. Encoded information is popular in various areas of graphic design because it is easy to use for the consumer and facilitates communication between producers and consumers of goods, services, advertising, etc. It also allows you to transfer an unlimited amount of information in digital form, with the help of interactive programs. 

After analyzing the samples of QR codes and AR codes presented in the article, we can conclude that they not only add value to product sales, but also have artistic value. After all, the design code by its structure includes external, internal and technical components (Fig. 18). 

The technical component assumes an unchanged structure. This is the program structure of the code that forms its constructive form. It is immutable, because it is a scheme, a code structure with thought-out technological features of manufacturing installation and operation. This form has an unchanging structure and is the first component when creating design code. 

The external component of the code is formed with the help of the artistic decision of the designer. Its meaning is represented by a visual image of the code, where the consumer acts as a viewer, and the graphic designer is the creator of the aesthetic form. It changes the appearance of the code so that the consumer pays attention to it as a complete art object. 

The internal component is a database that is generated every time the information is updated. This forms the inner part of the code, which carries the functional form, which is visually represented by the information component. This component can be seen only by decoding the code. 

As a result of the designer’s clear and thoughtful work, the constructive form is based on a functional approach to the structuring of the geometric elements of the code, and the aesthetic and functional 

**138** 

forms will be combined in a single compositional solution. Their interaction enables the graphic designer to develop a complete design code. The modification of each of the components provides a change and forms a new internal or external content of the code, which allows the graphic designer to create a unique work of art every time. 

Having studied coded information in various types of graphic design, we can conclude that the designer’s role in the development of coded information is to create an intuitive graphic object in which a digital code is present. A graphic designer creates a concept and develops a code so that a person, deciphering it, feels dynamic changes and becomes part of a design project. As a result, the consumer receives a positive experience of working with a digital tool of graphic design – coded information, and the designer – an exclusive design product. 

## **CONCLUSIONS** 

Coded information is a work of art and a powerful digital tool that allows customers to receive a variety of information on a product or service by simply scanning it with their smartphones. 

The significance of the use of QR-codes and AR-codes at the present stage of development is studied. It is proved that the close interaction of coded information and graphic design allows creating a “door” between the physical and digital worlds. It gives access to any information without direct contact with another person. Also, the use of coded information by leading brands in such types of graphic design as advertising, environmental, web, motion, print, packaging, set prop, mobile app, art and illustration is revealed. 

Coded information is perceived visually, and graphic design solves the problems of presenting an aesthetic and intuitive design product to the consumer: in addition to the visual image and text in graphic design, innovative methods are used. This allows using coded information as a new tool 

for brand transformation, as a component that increases the value of the product on the market. 

The codes contribute to the dissemination of information and allow the embedding of various data (i.e., files of any type, videos, URLs, pages, social network accounts, etc.). By using QR-codes and AR-codes, brands can improve the quality of customer service by providing them with additional information on the product they are trying to promote. It is proved that in graphic design it can be applied in the following niches: advertising, environmental, web, motion, print, packaging, set prop, mobile app, art and illustration graphic design. Almost all types of graphic design use both matrix codes and augmented reality tools, except the following niches: Environmental Graphic Design and Motion Graphic Design. Such niches currently use only QR-codes but given the rapid global development of innovative technologies and a society that is ready to bring them into their lives, they will also use AR-codes in time. 

The analysis is carried out and the systematization of the types of graphic design is made, which allows considering the coded information as a tool for brand transformation. The code is dynamic, so the coded information is always relevant, because it can be changed without losing functionality and change of appearance. 

By implementing digital tools of graphic design, namely coded information, the designer creates a positive experience for consumers. The viewer gets to know design objects not only with the help of thought, but also with the help of feelings, which are manifested only in the process of interaction with the design. Man focuses his attention on the digital code in order to find out “what’s in the middle?” and after decoding it becomes part of the design project, understands the concept of the project. In this way, dynamic changes occur and the idea of the graphic designer is realized. After all, its role is to create a complete “work of art”, where each element of the design is important in its formation. As a result, by following the basic components when 

**139** 

designing a design code, you can turn a standard matrix code into a work of art. 

The use of coded information by brands turns each consumer into a co-author because it requires interaction with it. It is easy to use, aesthetic, and accessible to everyone. Companies use augmented reality technologies in their codes, which is a trigger between the offline and online worlds. That is why coded information in graphic design does not lose its relevance and is rapidly evolving and penetrating everyday life. 

The conducted analysis showed that the design of digital codes has reached a level where some types of codes have become an object of art and are developed using various techniques, methods and design techniques. The perspective of further research is the study of the code as a structural set consisting of parts that are similar to itself, the study of the principles and compositional features of coded information from the point of view of design.

## **Santrauka** 

Straipsnyje atskleidžiamos užkoduotos informacijos grafinės kalbos ypatybės, identifikuojant, sisteminant ir derinant QR kodus ir AR kodus dabartiniame grafinio dizaino kūrimo etape.
